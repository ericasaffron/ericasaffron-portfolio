Crafting intuitive experiences, one pixel at a time.

I'm passionate about design because it transforms everyday interactions into seamless, frustration-free experiences.

My goal is to create user-friendly products that are not only functional but also genuinely enjoyable to use.

UX Designer and Researcher based in Brighton, with an MSc in Human-Computer Interaction Design from City St George's, University of London and a background in Computer Science and AI.

I have professional experience in UX and UI design, shipping interfaces for titles like FIFA Heroes, Warped Kart Racers on both iOS and Apple Vision Pro, and Need for Speed Unbound.

During my postgraduate studies I conducted user research across participatory design, information architecture, and interaction design.
